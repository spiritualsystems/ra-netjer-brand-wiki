## The teaching in one line

> *"How deep is your yes to your divinity?"*

Enlightenment is not a problem of knowledge, karma, or practice. It is a problem of **permission**. The yes is the hinge. (Sourced from Ra's 2026-03-16 public Transmission Satsang, opening frequency-setter — repeated 7+ times.)

## The three levels of yes

1. **Reluctant yes** — the teenager being dragged forward by the higher self. Enlightenment pulls, but the small self resists.
2. **Conditional yes** — "I'll say yes if X happens first." The yes exists but is hedged by circumstance.
3. **Deep resonant yes** — every fiber of being assents. This is a **choice available right now**, not a reward for sufficient practice.

The third yes is the one Ra teaches toward. Sustained practice is how a reluctant or conditional yes deepens into a resonant yes — not how enlightenment is "earned."

## Why permission is the mechanism

- **Old frame:** enlightenment is a destination reached through sufficient practice. You are insufficient until you arrive.
- **Ra's frame:** enlightenment is *already present* (this is Advaita). What blocks its expression is not reality but your **permission to see it**.

Implication: the whole path — teachings, retreats, mentorship, practices — exists to **remove permission barriers**, not to accumulate realizations.

## The filters companion teaching

Paired with the yes in the same satsang:

- **Filters = conditioning imposed on the enlightenment already present** (upbringing, society, trauma-adapted patterns)
- **Filters are not bad; they are tools.** They have a function — navigating the DMV, the workplace, strangers.
- **The practice is to make filters optional.** Remove them in safe spaces (meditation, sangha, intimate relationships). Reapply them when context requires.
- *"When you laugh, those filters disappear for a second."*

A deep yes **relaxes the filters** that have been holding the enlightenment underneath out of view.

## The yes is continuous, not once

From Ra's 2025-06-01 public teaching:

> *"You have to say yes again and again and again. Because it's not just once that you do it, but every moment in the now moment when that change is ready to happen."*

Beginnings are especially charged: each new threshold is a moment-of-power where the yes must be made fresh.

## Cautions

- **Not "say yes to manifest stuff."** The yes is to divinity, not to material outcomes.
- **Not forced affirmation.** Forcing a yes you don't feel just buries the reluctant yes deeper. Meet the reluctant yes honestly and let it ripen.
- **Not one and done.** A deep yes can shift back to conditional when circumstances change.
