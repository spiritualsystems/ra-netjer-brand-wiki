## The teaching

The Vedic teaching of **Prakriti and Purusha** — Nature and Consciousness, Creation and Creator — as the unifying principle for understanding *why* there is anything at all, and *how* realization closes the apparent gap between seer and seen.

## Stated directly

> *"The teaching in the Vedas, in the Hindu or Sanatana Dharma way of understanding this, is the principle of Prakriti and Purusha."*

- **Prakriti** — nature, creation, the manifest world, the dance
- **Purusha** — consciousness, the witness, the changeless
- They *appear* as two but are one: *"Prakriti and Purusha are one, appearing separate so they can have a dance."*

Ra uses **The Hall of Mirrors** as the central metaphor. At a carnival hall of mirrors, many reflections appear — each seems to be its own image. In truth, there is one source being reflected across many surfaces. The many are not different from the one; the one is not separate from the many.

> *"This is your creation, and it's here to serve you, it's here to do its job. This entire creation — its job is to be your mirror, because you have set out on this adventure of self-discovery, you have set out on this beautiful journey of knowing yourself through all these different reflections."*

## The signature formulation

> *"You are all this because you are all this because you are all because **you are the creator**."*

The repeated "all this" and "all" is a teaching cadence — the mind's first read is *I am all this stuff*, the second read is *I am that which is all this*, the third read is *I am the source of which this is creation*. Ra lets the sentence fold into itself.

## Why this teaching matters for Ra's body of work

Prakriti-Purusha provides the **cosmological root** for several of Ra's other teachings:

- **[You Are the Unknown](you-are-the-unknown.md)** — because you are the consciousness (Purusha) that is un-known even to itself
- **[Three-Step Process](three-step-process.md)** — Step 1 ("Oneness / Aham Brahmaasmi") is the Purusha recognition; Step 2 (release the story) is Prakriti moving without sticking; Step 3 (LFG) is the dance between them
- **[Stories, Hooks, Loops](stories-hooks-and-loops.md)** — because you are the space (Purusha) in which the stories (Prakriti) arise and dissolve
- **[Black Belt Framework](black-belt-framework.md)** — progressive realization of your own Purusha-nature while still participating in Prakriti's play

Understood together: *you are the witness that forgot itself into the play, and the play is your own dance arranged so you could remember.*

## Embodied delivery

Ra often delivers this teaching across three chakra meditations:

1. **Navel chakra** (seat of creatorhood, will)
2. **Heart chakra** (seat of love, of Shakti / Mahalakshmi — the divine feminine as Prakriti)
3. **Third eye chakra** (seat of the witness, Purusha)

The ascending chakra sequence *enacts* the teaching at the body level — beginning at the creator-will, rising through the love-play, arriving at the witnessing awareness.
