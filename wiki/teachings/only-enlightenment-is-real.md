## The teaching

Ra's most compressed nondual statement, delivered as the opening of a 14-minute public YouTube teaching (2024-12-30, "This Simple Realization Changes Everything About Existence"):

> *"Enlightenment is real. That's all there is. There's nothing else. There's no one else. You don't exist. I don't exist. Only enlightenment exists."*

He equates: **Enlightenment = God**. Both are the only real. Everything else is not false in an absolute sense, but non-permanent — not truly existent the way Enlightenment/God is.

The "simple realization": the entirety of existence — self, other, objects, stories, labels — are appearances on the surface of the one reality, which is Enlightenment/God. Seeing this, even intellectually, is described as freeing.

## Three registers Ra moves through

### 1. Phenomenological — going beyond stories

> *"What if, for one moment, you go beyond all stories? One moment where all these senses are purely there without the overlay of labels, without the overlay of judgements... What if you simply be? What if you simply exist? Without a story of existence. That is where you will find Zen."*

Same move as Summa Iru, beginner's mind, the "I don't know" mantra — different entry points to one shift.

### 2. Scientific — E=mc² as bridge

Ra uses Einstein's energy-mass equivalence as an intellectual scaffold, while being careful to note this is an *intellectual* approach:

> *"There is something even deeper, which can see beyond what the intellect can even hope to grasp. And that is an experiential realization of oneness, which cannot be really put into words."*

### 3. Practical — meditation as removal of the unreal layer

> *"Meditation is a coming back to who you are. And you are this reality already. And you have an overlay of the unreality that seems to be existing, overlaid on top of that. So, meditation is the removal of the layer of unreality."*

## The "depth perceives the depth" formulation

One of the video's most unusual phrases:

> *"There is a silence, a depth in which this is perceived by that depth itself. The depth perceives the depth, because the surface is no longer distracting it."*

Awareness recognizing awareness, not awareness recognizing an object.

## Infinity — not fullness but endlessness

Ra corrects a common spiritual frame — enlightenment as "fullness" (implying a finite full):

> *"This fullness is endless. It's not a finite quantity... It's not one, it's infinity. And it goes on and on. The depth is exploring its endlessness through the consciousness of itself."*

## Key phrases

- *"Enlightenment is real. That's all there is."*
- *"Only enlightenment exists."*
- *"What if you simply be? Simply exist. Without a story of existence. That is where you will find Zen."*
- *"The depth perceives the depth."*
- *"Meditation is the removal of the layer of unreality."*
- *"It's not one, it's infinity."*
