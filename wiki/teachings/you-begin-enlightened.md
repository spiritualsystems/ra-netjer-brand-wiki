## The teaching

> *"You begin enlightened. You're born enlightened."*

The starting state is not ignorance or darkness — it is pure light so complete that there is no contrast, nothing to compare, no self-recognition possible. Limitation, suffering, and forgetting arise not as punishment but as the **condition for self-recognition through contrast**.

Sourced from Ra's December 2025 Spiritual (R)Evolution keynote — a public keynote delivered at Sai Maa's event, where the framing "you are already enlightened, but forgot" is shared between the two teachers.

## Ra's formulation

> *"When you're in so much light, it's all bright and it's all wonderful. It's heaven. You are in this perfection. What's there to know? Everything is known... But also there's no contrast. There is so much light you cannot see anything. There's absolutely nothing to compare to. So we come into this seemingly difficult world."*

The Vedantic reference is explicit: *Brahma satya jagat mithya* — Brahman is real, the world is appearance. The appearance-world is not a mistake; it is a self-created dream whose purpose is the return to self-knowing.

## Seeker implication

This teaching reframes the seeker's situation from *"I lack enlightenment and must acquire it"* to *"I have always had enlightenment and must remember it."*

- Emotional register shifts from striving to recovery
- Urgency shifts from building to clearing
- Failure becomes impossible — the destination is the starting point
