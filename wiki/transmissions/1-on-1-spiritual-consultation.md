## What it is

A **free, application-based individual meeting** with Ra. Positioned on the website as:

> *"Get 1-on-1 guidance to find Clarity for your Spiritual path."*

Application calls-to-action:
- *"Apply for free Spiritual Consultation"*
- *"Apply for a 1:1 meeting with Ra"*
- *"Apply to meet Ra"*

## Actual structure

A Spiritual Consultation is **not a pure intake.** Ra runs a hybrid: intake + diagnostic teaching + energetic transmission + offer + soft enrollment.

A typical 45–70 minute call moves through roughly these phases:

| Phase | What happens |
|---|---|
| **Rapport & opening** | Greeting (Ra often opens in the seeker's language if he knows it); confirms recording; references prior contact |
| **Diagnostic intake** | Asks about profession and spiritual journey; listens. *"I would love to know your spiritual journey so far, what has happened in your life so far that has brought you to the path?"* |
| **Diagnostic teaching + energetic read** | Ra shifts from listening to teaching. Names what he sees: past-life patterns, current loops, hidden potential. Delivers transmission while speaking |
| **Future visioning + framework introduction** | *"What is your vision? How do you want to move forward?"* Then explains the Integrated Awakening Process, the weekly cadence, and the remove-limitations-and-activate-realization mechanic |
| **Program positioning** | Tier options, 6-month commitment window, "renovating a house" metaphor for depth of work |
| **Objection handling + trial structure** | If the seeker hesitates, Ra may offer a free guest session of the premium group tier; he probes beneath the surface to distinguish financial-timing from conceptual-resistance |
| **Logistics + warm close** | Walks through signup flow OR books a follow-up decision call |

Ra uses silence as a teaching tool. He uses the seeker's own words back to them as the reframe — if the seeker says *"I always hesitate,"* Ra's close might be *"that's the thing we're going to change"* rather than pushing them past their own hesitation.

## The intake voice

Ra's intake voice is more **diagnostic and present-tense-specific** than his website voice:

- Website: *"Integrated Awakening"* (abstract, branded)
- Intake: *"It's like renovating a house. You cannot start renovating one room and just like, okay, we go"* (concrete, relatable)

- Website: *"Discover your enlightenment"* (universal invitation)
- Intake: *"You have a magnetic quality in your voice that you're not using now"* (specific to the seeker in front of him)

Ra diagnoses patterns, names specific gifts, and drops small teaching transmissions even inside the first contact.

## The hidden thread in every consultation

Across consultations, **Ra's diagnostic read is the conversion lever** — not pricing, not credentials, not teaching content. The seeker is won by being *seen accurately by someone with authority.* The rest of the call executes on that recognition.

## Role in the offering stack

- **Gateway to mentorship.** A consultation is how a seeker moves from passive content consumption into direct relationship with Ra.
- **Standalone value.** Even seekers who do not enroll leave with real material — a pattern named, a reframe, a transmission. This is consistent with the non-transactional tone of the whole practice.
- **Filter + matching mechanism.** The application gate filters for seriousness; the consultation matches the seeker to the appropriate entry point (a specific tier, a guest session, or *"not the right time yet"*).
