## What it is

A **free introductory event** that functions as the lowest-commitment on-ramp into Ra's practice.

Featured on the Awaken With Ra website as an ongoing free event — the path for seekers who want direct exposure to Ra's teaching without any prior enrollment.

## Role in the offering stack

The Guest Pass sits below the 1-on-1 Spiritual Consultation in commitment depth. It is often also offered as a **free first session of the premium group tier** to prospective Integrated Awakening Program students after a consultation — so they can experience the program before committing to 6 months.

Two access paths:

1. **Open enrollment** via the website
2. **Post-consultation invitation** — offered during or after a [1-on-1 Spiritual Consultation](1-on-1-spiritual-consultation.md) to seekers who want to feel the premium tier before deciding

## What a seeker gets from a Guest Pass

- Direct in-session experience of Ra's teaching and transmission
- A sense of the community culture (*"Om Jai Jai Sai Ma,"* mutual-witness norms, hand-raising format — see [../community/i-am-community.md](../community/i-am-community.md))
- Material to decide whether to join the Free I AM Community and / or apply for a consultation
