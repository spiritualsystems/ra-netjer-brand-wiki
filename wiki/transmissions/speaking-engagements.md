## What it is

Ra is available as a **Keynote Speaker or Guest** on podcasts, summits, conferences, and online shows — positioned on the website alongside the mentorship and community offerings.

## Primary speaker topics

- **Awaken the Guru Within**
- **Night of the Soul**

These are Ra's two most developed speaking frames. Both translate his mentorship material into formats suitable for non-student audiences.

## Positioning for event hosts

Ra is positioned as an **Enlightened Transmission Teacher** whose work is grounded in:

- Direct experience of Dark Night and realization
- A living lineage (rooted in Advaita Vedanta, Buddhism, and Sanatana Dharma / Hindu traditions)
- A proprietary, structured methodology (the Integrated Awakening Process)
- Ordained monastic standing (Vajrayana Buddhist ordination)

Ra is **not** a motivational speaker or a "wellness" speaker. His register is reflective, grounded, and cosmological; his value for an event is the combination of authentic teaching, energetic transmission, and clear frameworks.

## Audience fit

Ra is most aligned with audiences of:

- Already-awakening seekers (not beginners)
- Spiritual-but-not-religious practitioners ready for depth
- Leaders, coaches, therapists, and practitioners who want to integrate spiritual realization with their work
- Communities inside or adjacent to non-dual / direct-path / yogic / Buddhist traditions
