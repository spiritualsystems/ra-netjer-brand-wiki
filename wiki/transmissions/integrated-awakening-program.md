## What it is

The **Integrated Awakening Program** is Ra's paid mentorship offering, built around the [Integrated Awakening Process](../frameworks/integrated-awakening-process.md). Students receive weekly sessions with Ra, practices and teachings, and full membership in the wider [I AM Community](../community/i-am-community.md).

## Tiered membership

The program is offered in tiers so that seekers at different stages and capacities can enter. Specific pricing and tier details are shared in a [1-on-1 Spiritual Consultation](1-on-1-spiritual-consultation.md) with Ra.

Across tiers, the consistent features are:

- **Weekly group session(s) with Ra** — teaching, transmission, and one-on-one-in-a-group mentorship
- **Access to the full I AM Community**
- **Sliding-scale accommodation is possible** for seekers whose financial situation requires it

The higher tiers include additional supporting sessions, including energy-healing transmissions rooted in Ra's family healing tradition in India.

## What students actually do

A typical group mentorship session opens with Ra teaching on the week's theme (for example, ego-arrest on the path, stories / hooks / loops, detachment + joy, forgiveness), then moves into hand-raising format where each student shares where they're stuck or what has shifted. Ra responds with:

- Reflecting the pattern back
- Naming the underlying structure (*"mass identification,"* *"the 108 post,"* *"hooks and loops"*)
- Prescribing first-principles counter-evidence work
- Holding silence as transmission

See [../frameworks/integrated-awakening-process.md](../frameworks/integrated-awakening-process.md) for the diagnostic moves Ra repeats.

Students work between sessions on practices Ra prescribes — meditation, inner work, energy work, interpersonal practices, and teachings on financial / material-life integration.

> *"It's very important to have practicality and spirituality together. If it's not grounded, it doesn't work."*

## Commitment window

Minimum **6 months.** Ra's framing:

> *"Six months commitment because we are doing deep work. You cannot start deep work. It's like renovating a house. You cannot start renovating one room and just like, okay, we go... there's a lot of deeper things, deeper past lives, traumas, healing of deep things that come out."*

## Guest pass / trial

Prospective students are often offered a **free first session** of the premium group tier so they can experience it before committing. Typically offered during or after a consultation. See [guest-pass-event.md](guest-pass-event.md).

## Who the students are

The program holds seekers across a wide range of stages — from students in early belief-dismantling to students navigating post-realization integration. Ra (and the community's culture of mutual witness) treats this range as accelerative, not distracting: everyone is in the same room, intentionally.

See [../seekers/who-ra-serves.md](../seekers/who-ra-serves.md) for who Ra specifically works with.
