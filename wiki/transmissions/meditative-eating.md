## What it is

**Meditative Eating** is Ra's signature embodied practice — a guided meditation woven around the act of eating. Featured on the Awaken With Ra website as *"Guided Meditation With Food."* It is one of the offerings included in the Free I AM Community benefits, and among the **earliest public Integrated Awakening offerings** (originally called *"Om To Yum"*).

Primary public teaching source: 2023-04-18 YouTube teaching "Eat Your Way to Spiritual Awakening" (38m54s) — Ra's most extended public articulation of the practice.

## The practice in brief

The practice turns the daily act of eating into transmission. Attendees join a live session (or use it as a solo practice), slow down, bring beginner's-mind attention to each sense engaged in eating, and allow the simple bodily experience of a meal to become a gateway to Divine Love.

## Why it exists as its own offering

Meditative Eating is a structural expression of several of Ra's teachings:

- **Spirituality and practicality together.** The cushion is not the only place of practice — the dinner plate is too.
- **Full-spectrum realization.** *"Bringing the light of enlightenment into everything — into everyday life, into work, into eating, walking, talking."*
- **Bhakti of eating.** Eating, when met with full presence, becomes a direct experience of Divine Love. This is a specific application of [bhakti](../teachings/bhakti.md) — the enjoyment of the divine through the ordinary.
- **Presence over abstraction.** Eating is somatic, daily, inevitable. A meditation anchored in eating is a meditation that cannot stay conceptual.

## Teaching elements from the 2023-04-18 source

- Each sense (taste, smell, texture, sight, sound of eating) engaged one at a time, as if for the first time
- Gratitude for the food as an act of recognition, not performance
- The mind's eating-on-autopilot as an obstacle — replaced with deliberate, slow engagement
- Eating as sacred ritual, not consumption
- The meal as an encounter with Divine Love through the body
