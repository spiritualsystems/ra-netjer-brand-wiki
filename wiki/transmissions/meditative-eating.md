## What it is

**Meditative Eating** is Ra's signature embodied practice, featured on the Awaken With Ra website.

From the website: *"Guided Meditation With Food."*

It is one of the offerings included in the Free I AM Community benefits list — a regular session format where Ra leads community members through a meditation woven around the act of eating.

## Why it exists as its own offering

Meditative Eating is a structural expression of several of Ra's teachings:

- **Spirituality and practicality together.** The cushion is not the only place of practice — the dinner plate is too.
- **Full-spectrum realization.** *"Bringing the light of enlightenment into everything, into everyday life, into work, into eating, walking, talking."*
- **Presence over abstraction.** Eating is somatic, daily, inevitable. A meditation anchored in eating is a meditation that cannot stay conceptual.

## Where it sits in the offering stack

Meditative Eating is listed among the Free I AM Community benefits. It is one of the recurring rhythms community members can participate in as part of the free tier.

## Related teachings

- [Roles as Path](../teachings/roles-as-path.md) — *"If you do anything in an enlightening way, that becomes the path."* Eating is a role; the enlightened orientation makes it a path.
