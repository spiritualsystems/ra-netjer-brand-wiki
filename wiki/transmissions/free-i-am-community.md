## What it is

The **Free I AM Community** is the free entry-point membership into Ra's practice. On the homepage it is positioned as Step 1 of a three-step path:

1. Join our I AM Community (Free)
2. Receive Enlightened guidance, practices & support
3. Walk your unique path to realize your Highest Potential

## What members receive

Per the website:

- Free Weekly Meditations on Zoom
- Energetically Supported Guided Meditations
- Q&A with Ra every week
- Live Zoom Classes
- Recorded Teachings (archive)
- Years of powerful transmission teachings
- Retreats & Spiritual Trips
- Guest teachings
- Book readings
- Meditative Eating sessions
- Voice & Chat support from Ra
- Loving community membership
- Various benefits based on donations to the non-profit

## Structure

- **Cadence:** weekly (meditations, Q&A, live classes)
- **Format:** Zoom-primary
- **Cost:** Free to join; funded by donations to the non-profit (Spiritual Leaders Institute)

## Role in the offering stack

The I AM Community is both:

1. **The on-ramp** — where Awakening Souls first meet Ra, experience his teaching style, and decide whether deeper work is right for them.
2. **The ongoing container** — for students of the paid Integrated Awakening Program as well. Mentorship is *in addition to*, not instead of, the community.

This is a both/and structure, not a funnel. The free community is not a lead-magnet with no substance — the site frames it as *"years of powerful transmission teachings"* available at the free tier.
