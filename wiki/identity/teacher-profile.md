## Who

**Ra Netjer** — ordained Buddhist monk (Vajrayana tradition) and Spiritual Awakening Mentor. Founder of the Awaken With Ra mentorship practice, under the Spiritual Leaders Institute (registered non-profit religious organization, California). Born in coastal Karnataka, India; currently teaches worldwide.

## Self-described title

**Enlightened Transmission Teacher.**

## Role & positioning

Ra positions himself specifically as a mentor to **Awakening Souls** — people already in some form of spiritual awakening, not beginners.

From satsang:

> *"I'm bringing to you who you are on your journey... for your first black belt... suitable for where you are at."*

Ra explicitly does *not* position as the highest teacher. He names himself a mid-level master and openly points seekers to his own gurus — past and present — as higher sources. This non-competitive framing is a structural feature of the practice. See [lineage.md](lineage.md) and [../teachings/black-belt-framework.md](../teachings/black-belt-framework.md).

## Stage of realization (self-described)

- **Enlightened** for 10+ years: *"more than 10 years now"*
- **Teaching** for 10-11 years: *"teaching around the same time, 10 or 11 years"*
- **Currently still a student** — actively learning from his current guru, Sai Maa
- **Identifies as:** *"Student forever."*

This dual teacher-and-student posture — authoritative in mentorship, openly learning from a higher-degree teacher — is one of the most distinctive features of how Ra holds his authority.

## North-star operating principle

> *"I just arrive and teach, which means everything else is taken care of. All the setup, all the things is taken care of. And I just do my best work. And then I have the time to relax and be receptive from life, to be in that state."*

## Core promise to seekers

> *"Your Enlightenment is possible in this lifetime! It will take time. It will take work. It will take a lot of letting go, but it's not as hard as you may imagine, because I'm with you every step of the way, guiding you to realize what you already are!"*

Three features of Ra's framing are visible in that line:
- **Enlightenment is achievable in this lifetime** (not deferred)
- **The path has real cost** (no toxic positivity)
- **The relationship is primary** — *"I'm with you every step of the way"*

## Teaching style at a glance

- **Martial-arts metaphor as central pedagogy.** See [../teachings/black-belt-framework.md](../teachings/black-belt-framework.md).
- **Diagnostic + prescriptive in mentorship.** Ra reads where a seeker is stuck, then prescribes specific practices.
- **Energetic transmission alongside language.** *"I'm working energetically with each person... it's much faster transformation than just speaking."*
- **Holds silence well.** Extended silences during teaching — silence itself is a teaching move.
- **Gentle but unflinching.** *"Really look at that. Really look at what I said."* Ra is not harsh, but will not let a student slide past their own pattern.

## On Ra's authority, in his own words

> *"Very powerful to have a fourth, fifth kind of degree black belt to teach you how to get your first degree black belt while also being in the lineage of multiple black belts, multiple enlightened masters."*
