## Who

**Sri Sri Sri Raghunath Guruji** — Ra's biological father and the anchor of the familial healing stream in Ra's lineage. Founder of **Sai Datta Peetha**, publicly known as the **Temple of Success**, in Bangalore, India (TempleofSuccess.org).

He left his body on or shortly before **September 1, 2022**, when his final darshan was held at Temple of Success. The public video documenting the ceremony was published on 2022-09-03.

## The healing tradition — Amruth Chikitsa

Raghunath Guruji revived and transmitted an ancient healing art and science called **Amruth Chikitsa** (Sanskrit: *amruth* = nectar/immortality; *chikitsa* = medicine/treatment). The work was guided by the blessings of **Shirdi Sai Baba**.

From the public video:

> *"Raghunath Guruji lived his life joyously helping more than a million people with health, happiness and peace of mind by reviving the ancient healing art and science of Amruth Chikitsa with the guidance and blessings of Shirdi Sai Baba."*

Shirdi Sai Baba is also the root lineage guru of Sai Maa — Ra's family healing tradition and his current guru's lineage share the same ultimate root. A significant lineage convergence.

## Origin story — businessman to healer

Raghunath Guruji began his adult life as a businessman. Under Shirdi Sai Baba's persuasion, he began offering healings in the small village of **Mugudti, Karnataka**. Initially only on Sundays, with no advertising — demand grew entirely by word of mouth. He eventually left business, relocated to Bangalore, and established Temple of Success. The center grew from a small 2-bedroom space to progressively larger buildings as hundreds of people came daily.

## The succession ceremony — September 1, 2022

Raghunath Guruji's **final darshan** was held at Temple of Success on Thursday, September 1, 2022, attended by hundreds of devotees and several interfaith dignitaries (Hindu swamis and Islamic leadership).

At this ceremony, **Ra was formally felicitated as the next lineage-holder of Sai Datta Peetha** under the name **Shakthi Kumar** — Sanskrit: *Shakthi* = divine power / feminine cosmic force; *Kumar* = son. The public video records this event.

This name was given to Ra in the Sai Datta Peetha lineage context. It predates the name "Ra Netjer," which was given later by Ra's Buddhist teacher.

## Ra's early formation under this lineage

From the video description (Ra's own authorship):

> *"Having grown up learning Amruth Chikitsa from his father, Ra began seeing the Gurus (Ascended Masters), energy, auras and chakras at the age of 9. He learned to move, transform and work intricately with energy with the intuitional guidance and blessings of the Gurus."*

Key biographical facts:

- Ra began perceiving Ascended Masters, energy, auras, and chakras at **age 9**
- He trained in Amruth Chikitsa under his father for several years in Bangalore
- He later adapted the work for Western audiences with the encouragement of his Buddhist teacher

## "Helped a million people"

This phrase appears in Ra's public teachings ("my parents helped a million people in India"). The 2022 darshan video is the primary source. The Amruth Chikitsa tradition was practiced jointly — Ra's mother (Gurumata, publicly known as **Jyothi Swarupa**) continues the practice today.

## Significance for the lineage record

1. **Shirdi Sai Baba as common root.** Raghunath Guruji's healing work was guided by Shirdi Sai Baba. Sai Maa's root lineage also runs through Shirdi Sai Baba. Ra receives both the family healing tradition and the Sai Maa teaching tradition — both rooted in the same field.
2. **Paternal Vedic-rishi ancestry.** Ra has spoken publicly about his paternal Sarasvata Brahmin lineage and Sanskrit/Vedic scholarly identity as *inherited*, not only acquired.
3. **Teaching-forward path.** Ra's current work is the teaching pathway (satsang, mentorship, retreats, the Integrated Awakening Program) via Awaken with Ra — not institutional administration of Temple of Success. The family practice continues in India via his mother and brother.
