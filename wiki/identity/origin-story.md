## Inherited spiritual field

Ra was born into a spiritual family in coastal Karnataka, India. Both his parents were Energy Healers. His father was recognized as a guru in the healing lineage:

> *"Thousands of people... even though he was not a guru in terms of somebody who brings you to enlightenment... His focus was on healing."*

Ra grew up inside this family tradition but chose a different path from his parents — *"the pathway to enlightenment"* rather than *"the pathway of healing."* His own orientation toward realization was present from early childhood:

> *"My aim since my early 20s was like, I want to go for full enlightenment... as far as I can, as deep as I can... 100%... if there is more than 100%, I want that too."*

## Childhood — Good Shepherd Convent

Ra attended a **Good Shepherd Convent school** in his early years — a Christian-mission school in India. The multi-tradition exposure (Christian school, Hindu home, early contact with ascended-masters lineage through his father) is part of why Ra's teaching fluently crosses traditions today. He has spoken publicly about seeing auras, energy, and Ascended Masters from age 9.

## Actor and photographer phase

Before the Silicon Valley chapter, Ra worked as an actor (lead film roles) and photographer in India. The 2024-06 Dream Catcher Podcast interview with Mark Rudd Larsen contains his most sustained public autobiographical account of this period:

> *"I had this entire wardrobe of things that I'm supposed to wear and look like a certain person because people have an expectation... even though I knew it was acting right, but at the same time, I just felt completely lost."*

The public-actor persona vs. private-spiritual-interior split was the immediate trigger for disconnection and depression. During one peak of DNOS Ra has named: *"I didn't have any reason to live... I wouldn't get out of my bed for four days at a time. I didn't eat, didn't even drink water."*

## 6-month CS in 20 days → Silicon Valley

From the actor phase, Ra pivoted to software. He has spoken publicly about completing a six-month computer-science program in 20 days — an expression of the warrior-energy pragmatism he would later teach. The CS chapter led to a Silicon Valley tech job.

> *"I quit my 6-figure Silicon Valley tech job, to mentor Awakening Souls towards Enlightenment."*

## Pre-guru awakening — Auroville

Before meeting his first guru, Ra received a significant healing in India:

> *"A particular healing that happened to me when I was ready... just before I met my first Guru... at Sri Aurobindo's place, Auroville, in India."*

This opened the way to the teachers that followed. See [lineage.md](lineage.md) for the full guru chain.

## Dark Night of the Soul

> *"Years of Dark Nights of the Soul — battling depression, utter hopelessness and meaninglessness."*

This is the crucible. The [Integrated Awakening Process](../frameworks/integrated-awakening-process.md) is, by Ra's own telling, extracted from this specific suffering — not theorized.

Ra has described the Dark Night as a **recurring, multi-layer pattern**, not a single episode. One chapter he has taught from publicly: a period in Mangalore where he began a daily slow walking meditation through the town:

> *"Every single day, I would wake up and start walking. Without thinking where I'm going, without planning. In this little town called Mangalore in South India. Not just normal pace — I would walk slow motion. I would walk very slowly. I would absorb every single thing that would happen all around me — all the sounds, all the sights, the motion of walking... In deep walking meditation. I didn't even know it was called walking meditation. I just had to find a way to stay in the present moment so fully by being in the body."*

The reframe that emerged from this period is foundational to the teaching:

> *"That heartbreak, that betrayal, that all the other circumstances that created the hell situation in my mind turned out to be the biggest spiritual blessing... I leveled up in my meditation, in my mindfulness, in my entire spiritual path... It was only conceptual knowledge. I had to apply it because without applying it, I was stuck in hell in my own head."*

This is the seed of the [Three-Step Process](../teachings/three-step-process.md) — the framework was forged in the Dark Night before it was named. The kundalini origin story Ra has referenced in public teachings is also tied to this Mangalore chapter.

## Pattern recognition at rock bottom

> *"In this rock bottom, I recognized that this suffering was actually a blessing that helped me see a repeating pattern... A pattern that I then saw with thousands of Spiritual seekers whom I have helped over the years."*

The framework is the externalization of this recognition.

## The calling

## Why this matters for positioning

The tech-industry-to-monastic arc is a distinct credential. But the *deeper* credential visible in Ra's own telling is the **inherited-lineage-plus-self-chosen-path** structure: Ra grew up in a spiritual family, and still had to find his own gurus and walk the Dark Night himself. He is not self-taught in a vacuum; he is also not following an inherited position. Both his authority and his humility have structural sources in the story.
