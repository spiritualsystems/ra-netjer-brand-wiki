## Role in the broader teaching

The YouTube channel is Ra's **public-facing teaching output** — distinct from the private mentorship container and from the polished website copy. It sits between: intentional and performed, but not corporate-polished. It serves as:

1. **The long-form teaching archive** — structured teachings on DNOS, mantra, beginner's mind, the Three Jewels, etc.
2. **Compressed teaching kernels** via Shorts — seed material for ongoing seeker exposure
3. **Public satsang transmission voice** — how Ra teaches to an audience that may or may not be in the mentorship container
4. **Lineage anchors** — the December 2025 keynote, the 2022 succession ceremony, the Tiruvannamalai satsangs
5. **Business-spirituality bridge** — podcast interviews (financial advisor, podcast hosts) in peer-audience register

## Earliest public satsang set — Tiruvannamalai July 2022

Eight satsang recordings from July 10–16, 2022 are the **earliest surviving public recordings of Ra teaching**. Held during a pilgrimage to Tiruvannamalai (Ramana Maharshi's town), at the Mountain Retreat Center and The Dreaming Tree café. The longest in the set is a 4h23m teaching titled *"How to overcome suffering?"*
