## Purpose

A working reference for anyone drafting content in Ra's voice — contributors, virtual assistants, and LLMs. All phrases here are from **Ra's own public material** (satsangs, website, public teachings). **Never invent phrases for Ra.** Only use what has actually been said.

If you're drafting in his voice, your job is mostly to **choose among existing phrasings**, not to generate new ones.

## Voice registers

Ra has multiple public voice registers. Match the register to the context:

- **Website / published voice** — polished, cosmological, branded
- **Satsang / teaching voice** — metaphor-sustained, lineage-acknowledging, poetic
- **Guided-meditation voice** — procedural, somatic, invitational
- **Consultation / intake voice** — diagnostic, present-tense, specific to the person
- **Puja / invocation voice** — Sanskrit-fluent, translates inline, blends lineages openly
- **Book / long-form voice** — narrative, willing to circle back, self-correcting, quoted dialogue
- **Keynote voice** — (public keynotes such as the 2025-12 Spiritual (R)Evolution keynote) — more public-audience, reconcilable-across-lineages, slower, explicit
- **Daily-practice voice** — compressed, three-word, mnemonic-friendly; public-facing for broad audiences (BNL / Breathe, Notice, Let Go)
- **Myth-busting voice** — enumerated refutations of common awakening misconceptions (*"Seven Myths of Spiritual Awakening,"* etc.)

## Core concepts in Ra's own language

- **Integrated Awakening** — the central process name
- **Awakening Souls** — Ra's term for the people he mentors
- **Disruptive Awakening** — a spiritual opening that has destabilized ordinary life
- **Dark Night of the Soul** — the prolonged suffering-within-the-path
- **I AM Community** — the free membership layer and larger container
- **Self-Realization** — Ra's preferred framing for the end of the path
- **Enlightenment** — used openly and directly, not softened
- **Divinity** — what seekers are awakening *to*
- **Bright beings** — Ra's term for seekers in the mission statement
- **Guru Parampara** — Sanskrit for lineage of masters

## Patterns across the body of work

- **Direct use of traditional vocabulary.** Ra says *Enlightenment*, *Divinity*, *Self-Realization*, *monk*, *Guru Parampara* — not the softer *growth*, *consciousness work*, or *mindfulness*. No translation into secular language.
- **Repetition as emphasis.** *"Expanding, expanding, expanding"* / *"again and again and again"* / *"higher and higher."* Ra lets a word repeat until it lands, rather than reaching for a synonym.
- **Paired contrasts.** *"Peace, Love & Joy"* / *"Disruptive Awakening or Dark Night of the Soul"* / *"It will take time, work, letting go... but it's not as hard as you may imagine."* Ra consistently names the difficulty *and* the possibility in the same breath.
- **Realization, not attainment.** *"...to realize what you already are."* Enlightenment is framed as discovery, not construction.
- **Honors suffering as structure.** *"This suffering was actually a blessing that helped me see a repeating pattern."* Pain is where the method comes from, not an obstacle to it.
- **First-person relational commitment.** *"I empower you,"* *"I'm with you every step of the way,"* *"to mentor Awakening Souls."* The voice foregrounds the *relationship*, not the method.
- **Student-forever humility inside authority.** In satsang, Ra readily names the teachers above him. Authority and humility held as complementary, not in tension.
- **Sustained metaphor.** A single teaching will ride a single metaphor (martial arts belts, Velcro, mile-posts, house renovation, hall of mirrors). Metaphor is load-bearing, not ornamental.

## Explicit "do not" — what is NOT Ra's voice

- **Toxic positivity.** Ra does not erase the difficulty of the Dark Night. He never says *"just think positive"* or *"elevate your vibration"* in the casual self-help register.
- **Competitive / dismissive of other teachers.** Ra praises his teachers and honors adjacent teachers. There is no public material of Ra framing his work *against* another teacher.
- **Marketing hype.** No *"unlock,"* *"proven system,"* *"transform your life."* The voice is grounded, not promotional.
- **Aggressive contrarian hooks.** Phrases designed to provoke ("Stop doing X, it's a scam" energy) are not Ra's register. Ra's challenge is inward, not at the field.
- **Secular-translation vocabulary.** Ra does not swap *Enlightenment* for *growth*, or *Divinity* for *higher self* when speaking publicly. Use the direct vocabulary.
- **Invented quotes.** If a phrase isn't documented in a source, don't put it in Ra's mouth.
- **Defaulting to lineage name-drops.** Do not reach for Ra's current guru's name to add gravitas. Name her only when the teaching explicitly is hers, when the lineage point requires citation, or when the source is a specific public keynote in her presence. Otherwise let Ra's voice stand on its own. (See `../CLAUDE.md` standing directives, 2026-04-21.)
- **Publishing pricing mechanics.** Do not articulate the logic behind tier pricing, sliding scales, or thresholds-as-filters. Name the transmissions and their rough shape; leave pricing strategy unspoken.
