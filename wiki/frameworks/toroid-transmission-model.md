## Headline

**Ra's field-geometry for how mantra-practice and teacher-student transmission actually work energetically: a self-reinforcing donut-shaped energy field (toroid) that strengthens through cyclical repetition, and pairs with a teacher's own toroid for inter-field amplification.** Public corroboration comes from Ra's 2025-10-27 mantra-practice teaching on YouTube.

## The core model — mantra as cycling toroid

Ra visualizes the geometry of mantra-practice as a toroid (donut-shape, self-returning field):

> *"It looks like a toroid — it's like a donut. There is something in the center, which is you. And there is so much upward energy that is being pushed up, that the energy is just moving up and that energy is coming back to you and then again coming into the cycle and moving up."*

### Why mantra must be cycled, not uttered once

Single mantra utterances don't build field-strength. Cyclical repetition is structurally required:

> *"Each time the energy flows like that is a cycle. And you're repeating it again and again and again — that has strengthened that toroid of gold light."*

Repetition is not emphasis-for-learning; it is **field-construction**. Each cycle adds to the standing wave.

## The paired-toroid transmission model (teacher-student)

> *"When you put two toroids next to each other, they are so good at working with other toroids — the energy movement can go between two toroids."*

**Implication:** transmission is not linear (teacher → student) but resonant. Each party maintains their own toroid. When brought into proximity, the toroids couple, and energy circulates *between* them. This explains:

- Why in-person transmission has different character than recorded transmission
- Why the student's own practice (their personal toroid) is prerequisite — a student with no toroid has nothing for the teacher's field to couple with
- Why deeper work requires foundational practice first — the foundation-work builds the student's personal toroid to sufficient strength

## The escape-velocity metaphor

Applied to how energy gets unstuck from source patterns:

> *"You need that escape velocity, which is much more than the gravity that can pull it back. It's like if you want to launch a rocket — you cannot just push it slowly out."*

There is a threshold below which energy falls back to the familiar pattern (gravity). Above threshold, it launches clear. The toroid gets the energy to escape velocity through repetition; the teacher's paired toroid can lend momentum to help the practitioner cross.

## Connection to mantra initiation

Mantras are **initiated** rather than simply read because the teacher's toroid is active during initiation — seeding the cycle-pattern in the practitioner's field so that their subsequent cycling has the right shape to build. See [Mantra Practice](mantra-practice.md).

## Connection to "okayness as charge-neutralizer"

> *"When you're in complete okayness you have no charge. So no matter how much charge the loop has, it doesn't have any sway on you."*

Loops are charged patterns attracted to charged identity; when the identity enters "okayness" (no charge), the loop's pull has nothing to grip. The mantra-toroid is what stabilizes "okayness" — it builds the non-charged standing wave.
