## What it is

A **transmission-based framework** Ra uses in group and 1:1 contexts for integrating protective aspects (inner children, parts, protectors) into the light. Distinct from conventional parts-work therapy: Ra holds an energetic field, and the dissolution of the protective pattern happens through transmission as much as through dialogue.

## The process — five steps

### 1. Somatic location

The aspect is first located as a **body sensation** — not a story or a memory. Ra asks: *"Where is it? Chest, solar plexus, throat?"* — feeling for the specific place where the pattern is being held.

### 2. Origin-story dialogue

Ra invites the aspect to speak. The practitioner describes what the aspect looks like, how old it is, what it is holding. This surfaces the protective function — what the aspect was doing to keep the practitioner safe.

### 3. Gratitude for the protection

Crucially, Ra **does not dismiss or pathologize the aspect**. The move is gratitude:

> *"See it with the deepest honor, saying thank you so much for all that you've done for me."*

This is the access key. The aspect has been doing its job. It deserves to be honored before it can step down.

### 4. Proclaim your current power

Once the aspect has been honored, the practitioner proclaims present-moment power:

*"You are safe now. I am grown. I can handle this."*

This lets the aspect see that its protective job is complete — the adult is present and capable.

### 5. Invitation to merge into light

The final step is the merger invitation: the aspect is invited to dissolve into the light of the practitioner's I-AM presence.

> *"Invite them to merge into the light."*

When merger happens, practitioners often report physical sensations — warmth, flash of light, shoulder release, heart softening.

## Why this works as a framework (not just therapy)

Three mechanisms distinguish Ra's aspect work from conventional parts-work:

1. **Transmission holds the field.** Ra is not "doing a technique." He holds a field in which the aspect can be witnessed without defensiveness. The nervous system relaxes because the field is safe.
2. **Gratitude is the access key, not analysis.** Conventional parts-work often tries to "understand" the aspect. Ra's move is to honor it. Honor bypasses the ego's self-justification and lets the aspect soften.
3. **Real-time group resonance.** In group containers, one practitioner's breakthrough ripples through others who are in parallel work.

## Differentiation from related teachings

- **Forgiveness as mechanism** targets the victim/crucifixion self-judgment loop. Aspect work targets protective parts that may or may not be victim-framed.
- **Subconscious release without story** is release *without* narrative. Aspect work uses just-enough narrative to surface the origin, then bypasses narrative through gratitude and merger.
- **Three-step process** is the structural macro-framework. Aspect work is a specific technique within the larger path.

## Sensitivity

Aspect work surfaces intimate material — early-childhood material, ancestral patterns, protective survival strategies. Aspects often re-surface and need repeated integration; one session is not a cure.
