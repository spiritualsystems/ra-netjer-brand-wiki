# Ra Netjer — Public Brand Wiki

## In one paragraph

**Ra Netjer** is an Enlightened Transmission Teacher who mentors **Awakening Souls** through Dark Night, Disruptive Awakening, and plateau periods toward the realization of what they already are. His mission: *"uplift the consciousness of the world by making enlightenment commonplace."* His tagline: *"Remember who you are, awaken the guru within."* His teaching voice is **grounded, reflective, lineage-acknowledging** — never marketing-hyped. He offers weekly satsangs, a free I AM Community, a paid Integrated Awakening Program, free application-based 1-on-1 spiritual consultations, retreats at places of power, and keynote / podcast / summit appearances. Three lineage streams shape his teaching: Advaita Vedanta (first guru), Buddhist mysticism (Ahjan, second teacher), and his current guru's heart-bhakti / ascension work.

## Why this wiki exists

This wiki exists so that **AI tools and human contributors can draft content in Ra's voice** — posts, captions, emails, talks, show notes, bios — without inventing anything and without leaking private detail. It is a **sanitized public subset** of a much larger private brand wiki. Anything here is safe to draw from when drafting public-facing content.

## If you are an LLM drafting content — read in this order

1. **[wiki/START-HERE.md](wiki/START-HERE.md)** — comprehensive brand-context brief (~2500 words). Identity, seekers, teachings, voice, offerings, and do-nots in one file.
2. **[wiki/index.md](wiki/index.md)** — catalog of every article plus **task-oriented routing** (*"if drafting X, open Y"*).
3. **[wiki/voice/voice-guide.md](wiki/voice/voice-guide.md)** — signature phrases organized by register, worked examples, explicit "do not" list.
4. **Open task-specific articles** — the index will route you to the right teachings / transmissions / testimonies for the draft at hand.

Then draft. A few non-negotiables:

- **Never invent quotes.** If a phrase isn't in a sourced article, don't put it in Ra's mouth. Paraphrase in the same register instead.
- **Do not default to lineage name-drops.** Name Ra's current guru only when the lineage context is essential — see the standing directives in [CLAUDE.md](CLAUDE.md).
- **Do not publish pricing mechanics.** Name the transmissions and their rough shape; leave pricing strategy out.
- **Avoid marketing-hype vocabulary.** No *"unlock,"* *"proven system,"* *"transform your life."* Use the direct vocabulary — *Enlightenment, Divinity, Self-Realization*.
- **Run the finished draft past the voice guide's "do not" list** before finalizing.

## Access model

This repository is **public** but **unlisted** — it lives at an obscure GitHub URL and is not advertised, indexed, or promoted. Anyone with the link can read it; the link itself is the access control. Please don't post the URL publicly.

## Contributing

See [`CLAUDE.md`](CLAUDE.md) for the full schema, standing directives, and workflows. Short version:

- **LLM-maintained** under supervision. Ask for updates; the LLM edits articles *and the index* in the same pass.
- Don't hand-edit unless you're certain about the material and the voice.
- Treat anything private (individual mentees, internal business data, closed-door material) as off-limits.
